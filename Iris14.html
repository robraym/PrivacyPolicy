Privacy Policy - Iris14

Iris14 is a watch face developed by Robraym for Wear OS devices, including Samsung Galaxy Watch models.

Iris14 does not collect, store, share, or transmit personal data from users.

This watch face does not require account creation and does not collect information such as name, email address, location, contacts, messages, photos, microphone input, camera data, health data, or any other personal information.

If Iris14 is downloaded or distributed through Google Play, certain technical information may be processed by Google Play and related services according to Google's own Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
